CAST

Coders Admission & Scholarship Test

Overview

Coding Blocks is pleased to announce the Coders' Admission & Scholarship Test (CAST) for Winter batches. Students appearing for CAST can win scholarships of up to a whopping 75% off the entire course fees of our foundation courses (Launchpad & Crux) at all centers. It will be an online aptitude test to check your logical ability.

The registration fees for CAST is ₹100, which will be fully adjusted against the course fee irrespective of scholarship offered.

When? Saturday, October 28 (8:00 PM to 9:00 PM)

Where? students.codingblocks.com (Online, from your home)

Syllabus? Aptitude & Logical Reasoning

Register Now

FAQ

(Drop a line at admissions@codingblocks.com if you have further queries)

  • Is it applicable only for foundation courses ?

    Yes, Scholarship test is only for Foundation Courses - Launchpad and Crux at Pitampura, Dwarka, Noida and Gurgaon Centers.

  • I have already enrolled for an August course, can I still get the scholarship ?

    Yes, you can still get the scholarship. We will evaluate your performance, and scholarship will be granted.
